## Identity is unique 
$$I_{1} \cdot I_{2} = I_{1} = I_{2} \text{ } \blacksquare$$

## Addition on Integers is associative
$$ \begin{align*}
&
(a+b) = a+b \\ &
 (a+b)-a = b\\ &\\
&
 \text{assume } (a+b)+c \neq a+(b+c)\\
&
(a+b)-a+c \neq (b+c)\\
&b+c \neq (b+c)\\
&b+c \neq b+c \to \text{contradiction}\\
&\therefore (a+b)+c = a+(b+c)&\blacksquare 
\end{align*}$$
