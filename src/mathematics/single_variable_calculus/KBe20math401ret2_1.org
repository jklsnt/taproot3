   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   f(a) &= 1\\
   g(a) &= 0\\
   f(b) &= 0\\
   g(b) &= \frac{\pi^3}{8} > 1\\
   \end{aligned}
   $$
   Because these functions each traverse $0 \le y \le 1$ over the interval $0 \le x \le \frac{\pi}{2}$ in opposite directions and are continuous over that range, they must cross somewhere in that range.
