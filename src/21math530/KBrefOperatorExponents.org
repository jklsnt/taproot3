#+TITLE: Powers of Operator maps
* \(T^m\)                                                               :def:
  #+begin_quote
  Supppose $T \in \mathcal{L} (V)$ and $m$ is a positive integer.

  \[\begin{aligned}
  T^m = \underbrace{T\cdots T}_{\text{ $m$ times }}\\
  T^0 = I\\
  T^{-m} \text{ for an invertible map $T$ } = \left( T^{-1} \right) ^m
  \end{aligned}\]
  #+end_quote
* results
** exponent rules work
*** $T^mT^n = T^{m+n}$
*** $\left( T^m\right) ^n = T^{mn}$
