# conjugate transpose, v\* [[def]{.smallcaps}]{.tag tag-name="def"} {#conjugate-transpose-v}

The conjugate transpose ($v^*$) unary operator means you conjugate a
vector and then take the transpose

$$\begin{aligned}
  v^* = \overline{v}^\top
  \end{aligned}$$
